About Us

Welcome to PlantHerPower, where eco-feminism meets ethical fashion. Founded by Kashif and his girlfriend Tooba, our brand combines a deep love for nature with a passion for women's empowerment.

We believe that fashion should be a force for good – celebrating the strength of women while honoring our planet. Every design tells a story of growth, resilience, and the beautiful connection between feminine power and the natural world.

Our mission is to create clothing that not only looks beautiful but also carries a message of sustainability, empowerment, and positive change.

Our Promise

Eco-Friendly Printing

All products are printed on demand using environmentally safe, water-based inks that are gentle on both skin and planet.

Global Shipping

We ship worldwide through trusted partners, ensuring your order reaches you safely no matter where you are.

Zero Waste

Made to order means no excess waste – each piece is created specifically for you, reducing environmental impact.

Full Tracking

Every order comes with complete tracking information so you can follow your package from creation to delivery.

Sustainable Packaging

Your order arrives in biodegradable or recycled packaging materials, keeping our commitment to the earth.
